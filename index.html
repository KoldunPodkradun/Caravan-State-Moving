About our service

Caravan State Moving is a family-owned and operated moving company located in Pasadena. We offer full-service moving for individuals and businesses. Our first-rate services include quilt-pad wrapping of your furniture, disassembly and reassembly, and the option to leave clothing in your dressers.

Local Moving

With a reputation for quality in our industry, our trustworthy movers can handle your move quickly, efficiently, safely, and economically. Whether you need to move a few pieces of furniture to an apartment, a fully furnished house, or all of your belongings into a high-rise building, we’re equipped to make your move happen.

Long Distance

In addition to our various types and sizes of local moving services, we offers long distance moving services. Wherever you’re moving within the U.S., our fully licensed and insured moving company has the manpower and resources designed to handle the special requirements of long distance and interstate moves.

Storage Services

If you are ‘between places’ due to closing dates, renovations, or long term travel, we can provide a storage solution that works for your budget as well as your belongings. We will always be ready to assist you with a plan that will alleviate the headaches of entrusting your goods on a temporary basis.
